About

Hello

My name is Faris Halepovic, I am 20 years old and I am doing this project as part of my studies at the MMI department in Montbéliard.

The Notes for later project aims to leave a fragment of memory of our time. So that future generations can have a glimpse of what the world was like in our time.

In this project we were asked to present a film, a book, a music, a place, a fear and a hope.

For the film, I chose The Batman which is a superhero film directed by Matt Reeves, which I enjoyed immensely.

For the book, I chose the comic Batman year one written by Frank Miller and drawn by David Mazzucchelli, this comic book retraces the origins of Batman, it's also the comic book I advise to start with to discover Batman as a whole.

For the music, I chose the song It was a good day by Ice Cube, for me this song is close to perfection and is one of the few songs that fits any time of the day.

For the location, I chose Manhattan, because it is a city I would love to visit, it has a different charm from other big cities with its countless skyscrapers that make you lose your mind.

For fear one of the things that terrifies me the most is that one day we will lose control over new technologies and more specifically over AIs.

The hope I have for the future is that we will eventually find real solutions to stop global warming.
