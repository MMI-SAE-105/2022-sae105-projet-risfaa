Glossary

ADEME :
Agency for Environment and Energy Management.
GTA IV :
Grand Theft Auto IV is an open-world action-adventure video game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games.
Thriller :
A novel, play or film with an exciting plot, usually involving crime or espionage.
For example: "a tense thriller about a diamond heist gone wrong".
Box office :
A scale of success for a film based on the amount of money made.
Pègres :
A world of thieves, crooks forming a kind of class.
DC comics:
One of America's leading comic book publishers, the initials DC are an abbreviation for Detective comics.
Lyriciste :
Lyricist, writer of song lyrics.
